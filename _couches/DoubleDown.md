## About
We’re building the **First Toronto Hacker House.**

Hacker houses originated in **San Francisco**, where many Canadians go to immerse themselves in a highly innovative environment. However, San Francisco comes with major barriers: extremely high rent, visa requirements, and an intense, high-pressure culture.

Our goal is to make that same collaborative, fast-moving environment accessible right here in **Canada’s largest city**.

Some of the themes for this hacker house are
> Software · AI · Video Game Development · Web Development · Blockchain · Open Source
